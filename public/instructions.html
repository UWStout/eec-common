Welcome to Karuna

Welcome to the development server for the Karuna EEC tool! From here, you can download and install the latest version of the Chrome Extension Alpha. You will also find instructions for installing and activating the extension.

Karuna uses a Chrome browser extension to assist with team communication. The extension must be installed and active in your browser for the full Karuna experience. You must also access your team communication tools through their web browser interfaces.

Download Karuna Extension
  • Click the above button to Download the extension archive.
  • Locate the archive on your computer (look in your 'downloads' folder)
  • Extract all files from the archive to a folder on your computer (fig 1):
    1. Select the archive zip file
    2. Click 'Compressed Folder Tools'
    3. Click Extract all and point it to a reasonable location on your computer

The extension will eventually be available in the Chrome Web Store but during the Alpha phase of development, you must install the extension manually. NOTE: Make sure you have downloaded and expanded the extension archive first (see above).

  • Open Chrome and visit the URL chrome://extensions (fig 2)
  • In the upper right, toggle the 'Developer Mode' to the on position
  • Click the 'Load Unpacked'
  • Navigate to the expanded extension folder on your computer (fig 3)
  • Click 'select folder' to load and activate the extension
